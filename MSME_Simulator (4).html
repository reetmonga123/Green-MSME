Economic Concepts Behind This Simulator

🏦

RBI Monetary Policy

The RBI repo rate is the rate at which commercial banks borrow from RBI. Higher rates → costlier loans → MSME credit constraint → slower expansion. Modelled as: IC = 0.4I × rRBI

📉

Inflation Impact

Rising inflation erodes real profits. MSMEs face cost-price squeezes when input costs rise faster than output prices. Simulator applies 50% pass-through: IE = GP × (π/2)

🌱

Green Economics

Sustainability adoption reduces long-run costs via energy efficiency (10% saving for high-level adopters) and attracts ESG credit. Modelled as annual growth bonus: g ∈ {0%, 4%, 9%}

📊

MSME & GDP

MSMEs contribute ~30% to India's GDP, employ 11 crore workers, and form 48% of exports. FHS = composite index of profitability + policy + sustainability + inflation resilience.
